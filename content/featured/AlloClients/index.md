Architected and built the entire multi-tenant SaaS ecosystem for independent therapists — from scratch. A complete digital ecosystem consisting of three interconnected applications: a public booking directory (<a href="https://allo-therapeute.fr" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">allo-therapeute.fr</a>), a marketing platform (<a href="https://allo-clients.com" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">allo-clients.com</a>), and a practitioner SaaS management app (<a href="https://app.allo-clients.com" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">app.allo-clients.com</a>). Features include automated appointment scheduling, complex RBAC, real-time data synchronization, and a built-in analytics system.
