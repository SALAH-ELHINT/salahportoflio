<strong>1st Prize MoroccoAI Hackathon 2022</strong> at Université Hassan II.<br/><br/><strong>Problem:</strong> African government procurement contracts (tenders) were scattered across hundreds of sources with no unified, structured way to access or analyze them.<br/><br/><strong>Solution:</strong> Built an automated pipeline that <strong>scrapes, structures, and analyzes</strong> public procurement data across Africa using Python and Selenium. Applied <strong>NLP techniques</strong> to extract entities, classify contract types, and surface actionable insights making previously inaccessible tender intelligence available in a clean, queryable format for businesses and financial analysts.
